Method 1: Sports Hub Screen Share Studio

Built In

What You Need

  • Desktop browser (Chrome or Edge recommended)
  • YouTube stream key
  • FFmpeg installed on the Sports Hub server

Step-by-Step

  1. Open /screen-share from Sports Hub.
  2. Select your match and open the overlay or score desk in a new tab.
  3. Click Start Screen Capture and choose the tab/window to share.
  4. Paste your YouTube stream key.
  5. Click Go Live To YouTube.
https://your-app.onrender.com/screen-share

Note: this method replaces Streamlabs. Your browser captures the screen, and Sports Hub relays it to YouTube.

Method 2: iPhone Camera Stream

No Computer Needed

What You Need

  • iPhone with iOS 15+
  • YouTube app with live access enabled on your channel
  • Your Sports Hub URL

Step-by-Step

  1. Create a match and manage scoring from /desk.
  2. Open your overlay page on iPhone:
https://your-app.onrender.com/overlay?id=1
  1. Tap Start Camera on the overlay page.
  2. Start a live stream from the YouTube app.
  3. Use iPhone screen share to broadcast the overlay + camera composition.

Method 3: OBS Studio (Computer)

Professional Quality

What You Need

  • Computer with OBS Studio
  • Camera source (webcam, phone webcam app, or capture card)
  • YouTube stream key

Step-by-Step

  1. Add camera source in OBS.
  2. Add Browser Source with this URL:
https://your-app.onrender.com/overlay?id=1&nobg=1&minimal=1
  1. Set Browser Source to 1920x1080.
  2. Paste your stream key in OBS stream settings.
  3. Start streaming.
